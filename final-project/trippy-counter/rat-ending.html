You request for your hot dog to be "drowned" in ketchup. The cashier complies and covers every square inch of the hot dog.
She tells you,
"that'll be $5.50"
You hand her about six bucks, and she in turn, places it in the register and gives your change of 50 cents.
You gladly take the plain hot dog and begin devouring it right then and there.
The texture is less than desirable with it's chewiness somewhere between a slim jim and a cork from a wine bottle. However the taste was a different story...
You're not sure how the actual hot dog tastes due to the Ketchup covering it up each time you take a bite, but hey, it's good ketchup! Although the ketchup seems to be quite different than what you're used to...
"Oh yeah, that's the owner's personal recipie, it's pretty nice!"
Completely caught off gaurd by this new voice, you swing your head towards it. You see a three dancing rats. The middle rat addressing you in particular,
"You might wanna be careful, cause that ketchup has been sitting there fermenting for a while now."
"I've heard that you start hallucinatin' if ya have it with a couple of drinks!"
You notice that the cashier hasn't moved in a while as if she was frozen in time.
"Don't worry, it wears off after a while. Maybe try dancing it off will help?"
Seeing as you proably don't have any other choice, you join the rats in a dance that lasts through the night.
(You got the Trippy Ending!)
